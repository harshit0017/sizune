About the job
Job Title: Vice President, IT Operations
Location: 3x per week on-site in Philadelphia (Center City)
Duration: Direct hire, Permanent 

Compensation: Up to $275,000 + Performance Bonus + Great benefits/PTO 

Exact compensation may vary based on several factors, including skills, experience, and education.



Job Summary: Lead and oversee the firm's IT core technology platforms, including infrastructure, software solutions, custom development, enterprise systems, data management, and IT service management. Ensure the reliability, security, performance, and scalability of IT systems.


Key Responsibilities:

Align IT platforms and systems with business goals to drive user satisfaction and operational efficiency.
Analyze user feedback to identify trends and improve satisfaction metrics.
Partner with the VP of User Experience for exceptional service delivery.
Collaborate with Security Operations and IT Risk teams to ensure system security and compliance with standards (ISO27001, SOC, etc.).
Establish and monitor KPIs for IT operations, reporting to senior leadership.
Recruit, mentor, and develop a high-performing IT team.
Manage IT budgets and vendor relationships for cost-effective solutions.
Ensure timely and successful delivery of technology projects.
Communicate complex technology solutions to executives and business leaders.


Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in IT, Computer Science, or related field (Master's preferred).
Minimum 10 years of IT management experience.
Strong customer service, problem-solving, and decision-making skills.
Proven experience with infrastructure, cloud services, data management, and enterprise applications.
Knowledge of international security and privacy standards (ISO27001, SOC, HIPAA, GDPR).
Familiarity with software development methodologies (Agile, Scrum).
