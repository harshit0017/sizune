Description

The Director of Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Services is a critical leadership role responsible for the overall planning, implementation, and management of the organization's cybersecurity strategy and IT infrastructure. This includes protecting sensitive information, ensuring business continuity, and maintaining a secure and efficient technology environment. The Director will lead a team of cybersecurity and IT infrastructure professionals, providing guidance, mentorship, and development opportunities.

Strategic Leadership And Vision

Develop and execute an enterprise data strategy that aligns with Tutor Perini’s long-term business objectives and market positioning.
Lead the company’s Cybersecurity and Infrastructure strategy, focusing on enterprise cybersecurity and infrastructure solutions, standards and automation to enhance operational efficiency, reduce costs, and improve project timelines.
Build partnerships with executives, divisional leaders, and project managers to ensure cybersecurity and infrastructure solutions support strategic goals across all levels of the organization.

Cybersecurity And Infrastructure Strategy & Governance

Oversee the design and implementation of robust platforms to ensure data quality, accessibility, consistency, and privacy.
Develop and implement a comprehensive cybersecurity strategy aligned with industry’s best practices and regulatory requirements
Lead the organization's incident response team, ensuring preparedness for and effective response to cybersecurity incidents.
Conduct regular security assessments, vulnerability scans, and penetration testing to identify and mitigate risks.
Oversee the implementation and management of security tools and technologies, including firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, endpoint security, and data loss prevention solutions.
Stay abreast of emerging cybersecurity threats and vulnerabilities, proactively adapting security measures to address new challenges.
Assists in the evaluation and creation of hardware standards for all aspects of the IT infrastructure and Security.
Oversee the design, implementation, and maintenance of the organization's IT infrastructure, including network infrastructure, servers, storage, cloud services, and end-user computing.
Ensure the availability, reliability, and performance of IT systems to support business operations.

Operational Excellence

Drive the implementation of data-driven processes across construction, procurement, and engineering teams to improve decision-making and operational efficiency.
Identify opportunities for process automation and optimization using AI and robotic process automation (RPA) tools to enhance project delivery and cost management.
Ensure that cybersecurity and infrastructure solutions deliver measurable value by monitoring key performance indicators (KPIs) and aligning with industry benchmarks.

Team Leadership & Development

Build and lead a high-performing team of cybersecurity, networking, identity and system engineers and administrators to execute the company’s cybersecurity and infrastructure strategy.
Support a data-driven culture across the enterprise, ensuring that employees at all levels understand the value of data and are empowered to make informed decisions.
Develop a talent pipeline by mentoring and training IT professionals to support Tutor Perini’s growth and innovation goals.
